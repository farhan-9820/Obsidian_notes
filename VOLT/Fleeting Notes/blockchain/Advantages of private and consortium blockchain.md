
- **Lower Transaction Costs:** Transactions only need to be verified by a few high-processing nodes, rather than tens of thousands of consumer laptops.
    
- **Higher Speed and Throughput:** Fewer nodes and controlled environments mean vastly faster block times and clearing speeds.
    
- **Reversibility and Rule Flexibility:** If a mistake or hack happens, the consortium or company can easily step in, modify balances, or revert transactions.
    
- **Enhanced Privacy:** Because read access can be strictly restricted, sensitive corporate or personal data isn’t broadcasted to the entire world.
