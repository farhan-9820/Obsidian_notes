
a mathematical algorithm that takes an input of **any size** (a single letter, a sentence, or an entire book) and turns it into a fixed-size string of characters.

In most major blockchains (like Bitcoin), the standard algorithm used is [[SHA-256]] (Secure Hash Algorithm 256-bit). No matter how big or small your input is, a SHA-256 hash will always be exactly 64 hexadecimal characters long.
