The main Bitcoin blockchain (Layer 1) can only process about 7 transactions per second. This is nowhere near enough for a global population to trade daily.

To solve this, the industry uses **Layer-2 networks**, most notably the **Lightning Network**.

- **How it works:** Think of the main Bitcoin blockchain like a high-court property ledger, and the Lightning Network like a running bar tab. Millions of people can trade fractions of Satoshis back and forth instantly and nearly for free off the main chain.
    
- **The Settlement:** When people are done trading, the "tab" is closed, and the final balances are bundled together and written onto the main Bitcoin blockchain in a single transaction.
    

This allows billions of people to conduct daily trades simultaneously without clogging the main system.
