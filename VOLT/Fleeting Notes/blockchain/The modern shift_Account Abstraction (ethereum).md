

For a long time, the strict divide between human accounts (EOAs) and contract accounts caused friction—losing a private key meant losing everything.

The network relies on **Account Abstraction** (standards like ERC-4337 and EIP-7702). This essentially bridges the gap, allowing users to turn their everyday wallets into mini smart contracts. This allows for features like logging into a crypto wallet with WebAuthn/passkeys (FaceID), letting apps sponsor your gas fees so you don't need ETH to start, or automating recurring subscription payments natively on-chain
