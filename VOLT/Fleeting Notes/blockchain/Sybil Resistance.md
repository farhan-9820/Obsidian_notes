a **Sybil Attack** is when one person creates thousands of fake, anonymous identities (like creating 10,000 fake Twitter accounts) to manipulate a system or outvote everyone else.

If a blockchain counted votes by IP address or "one user account = one vote," a hacker could easily spin up 1 million fake virtual computers and rewrite history.

Proof of Work ties "voting power" directly to **laws of physics and thermodynamics (computing power and electricity)** instead of digital identities.

- You cannot fake or clone a computer chip.
    
- You cannot fake the electricity required to run that chip.
    
- In PoW, your "vote" is measured in **hashes per second** (how much raw math your hardware can calculate).
