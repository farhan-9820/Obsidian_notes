

#### **Two-Tailed (Non-Directional)**

You are looking for _any_ difference, regardless of whether it’s higher or lower.

- **Notation:** $H_a: \mu \neq \mu_0$
    
- **Example:** "The new energy drink changes heart rate" (could be an increase or a decrease).
    

#### **One-Tailed (Directional)**

You are looking for a specific direction of change.

- **Notation:** $H_a: \mu > \mu_0$ (Right-tailed) or $H_a: \mu < \mu_0$ (Left-tailed).
    
- **Example:** "The new energy drink **increases** heart rate."
